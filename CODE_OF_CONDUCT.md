# Code of Conduct

We value a diverse, inclusive, and respectful community.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and considerate.
- No harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Communicate openly and kindly.
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening an issue or contacting the maintainers at your-email@example.com.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in warnings or removal from the community to maintain a safe space for everyone.
